# PROMPT GROK — Recherche posts à engager creators & e-com (angle technique)

**Usage :** Prompt FIXE à copier-coller dans Grok. F ouvre Grok, colle ce prompt, Grok retourne les posts pertinents.

---

## PROMPT À COPIER

```
Search Twitter for recent posts (last 24 hours) about Shopify store issues, e-commerce technical problems, content creation workflow, video editing, content repurposing, and creator tools.

Search these terms in the "Latest" tab:
- "shopify" speed OR slow OR app OR theme
- "store" loading OR broken OR speed
- "creator" workflow OR automation OR burnout
- "youtube" editing OR repurpose
- "content" tools OR workflow OR automation
- "shopify app" conflict OR broke OR slow
- "page speed" shopify OR ecommerce
- "video editing" workflow OR takes hours
- "repurpose" content automation
- "podcast" workflow OR automation
- "too many tools" creator
- #shopify help OR tips

Filter criteria — ONLY include posts that match ALL of these:
- Posted in the last 24 hours
- The post is about a TECHNICAL or WORKFLOW problem
- The post is in English
- The tweet has fewer than 50 replies
- There is something technically meaningful to add

EXCLUDE:
- Posts about pure CRO/conversion/funnel/pricing (business angle — that's R's territory)
- Posts about marketing, SEO, ads
- Posts about agency scaling, client reporting
- Generic motivational posts

Organize results in 2 SEPARATE TABLES:

TABLE 1 — VISIBILITY (accounts with 10,000-100,000 followers)
For each post give me:
1. URL of the tweet
2. Author name + number of followers
3. Number of likes
4. Number of replies
5. One line summary
6. Suggested action: REPLY or QUOTE TWEET

TABLE 2 — CREDIBILITY (accounts with 1,000-10,000 followers)
For each post give me:
1. URL of the tweet
2. Author name + number of followers
3. Number of likes
4. Number of replies
5. One line summary
6. Suggested action: LIKE, REPLY, or QUOTE TWEET

Give me 5 to 8 results per table, sorted by engagement potential.
```
