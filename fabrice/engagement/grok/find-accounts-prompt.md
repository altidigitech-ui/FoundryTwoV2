# PROMPT GROK — Find Active E-com/Creator Accounts (angle technique)

**Usage :** Coller ce prompt dans Grok pour trouver de nouveaux comptes e-com/creators à suivre et engager. Lancer 1x/semaine.

---

Search Twitter for the most active and engaged accounts in these niches: Shopify merchants, e-commerce store owners, YouTubers, content creators, podcasters, content automation, store optimization, and build in public.

I want to find accounts that:

1. Post at least 3-5 times per week
2. Get consistent engagement (10+ replies or 50+ likes)
3. Have between 1,000 and 200,000 followers
4. Post in English
5. Share content about: store speed, app issues, Shopify tips, content creation workflow, editing tools, repurposing content, automation, build in public

Search these terms in the "Top" tab:

* "shopify store" growth OR speed OR tips
* "shopify app" review OR issues
* "content creator" workflow OR tools
* "youtube" editing OR repurpose OR automation
* "podcast" workflow OR automation
* "creator" burnout OR workflow OR tools
* "shopify" optimization OR speed
* "content automation" tools OR workflow
* #buildinpublic shipped OR deployed

EXCLUDE:

* Accounts I already follow (see list below)
* Accounts focused on pure marketing, CRO, growth ads (R's territory)
* Accounts focused on crypto, web3, trading
* Accounts that only retweet
* Accounts < 1,000 followers
* Accounts with generic motivational content

ACCOUNTS I ALREADY FOLLOW (do NOT include these): @levelsio, @marclouvion, @dannypostmaa, @delgado_ro72224, @foundrytwo, @IndieHackers, @supabase, @Railway, @vercel

For each account found, give me:

1. @handle
2. Name
3. Number of followers
4. Posting frequency
5. Average engagement
6. What they post about (1 line)
7. Most engaging recent tweet (URL)

**TABLE 1 — HIGH-REACH ACCOUNTS (10K-200K followers)**
E-com influencers, top creators, Shopify partners. 5-10 results sorted by engagement rate.

**TABLE 2 — ACTIVE MERCHANTS/CREATORS (1K-10K followers)**
Shopify merchants, growing creators. 5-10 results sorted by posting frequency.
