# PROMPT GROK — Recherche cibles cold outreach creators & e-com (angle technique)

**Usage :** Prompt FIXE à copier-coller dans Grok. F ouvre Grok, colle ce prompt, Grok retourne les cibles.

---

## PROMPT À COPIER

```
Search Twitter for recent posts (last 48 hours) from content creators (YouTubers, TikTokers, podcasters) and Shopify merchants who are:

- Complaining about editing time or workflow complexity
- Struggling with repurposing content across platforms
- Frustrated with slow Shopify stores or app conflicts
- Looking for automation tools or workflow improvements
- Burned out from manual content production processes
- Having theme bugs or checkout issues on Shopify

Search these terms one by one in the "Latest" tab:

- "editing takes" hours OR forever
- "repurpose content" workflow
- "youtube burnout" OR "creator burnout"
- "too many tools" creator
- "shopify slow" OR "store speed"
- "shopify app" broke OR conflict
- "content automation"
- "video editing" workflow
- "podcast" workflow OR automation
- "theme" shopify problems OR bugs
- "page speed" shopify
- "creator" workflow nightmare

For each relevant post found, give me:

1. URL of the tweet
2. Number of followers of the account
3. Number of replies on the tweet
4. Vertical: creator OR e-commerce
5. One line: what they're struggling with technically

Filter criteria — ONLY include posts that:

- Posted in the last 48 hours
- Account has 50-50,000 followers
- Tweet has fewer than 50 replies
- Post is in English
- Post is about a WORKFLOW/TECHNICAL problem (not a business/revenue problem)

EXCLUDE:

- Marketing agencies or freelance marketers (that's R's territory)
- Posts about chargebacks, ROI, client reporting (business angle = R)
- Developers discussing pure code architecture
- Accounts selling competing SaaS tools
- Posts from accounts I already follow: @delgado_ro72224, @foundrytwo

Give me 10 to 15 results, sorted by most recent first.
```

---

## NOTES

- F LEAD les Content Creators. La majorité des résultats devraient être des creators.
- Les merchants e-com sont complémentaires (angle technique, pas business).
- Différence avec R : R dit "you're losing $2,100/month". F dit "the bottleneck is 14 apps each injecting their own JavaScript".
