### MERCREDI 18/03 — ⏳ À COMPLÉTER AVEC DONNÉES POST-LANCEMENT

Titre : 40 audits before launch. Here's the data.

We launched Leak Detector on March 16. An AI tool that audits landing pages across 8 conversion categories in 60 seconds and returns a score out of 100 with specific fixes.

Before going live, we ran 40 audits to stress-test the tool and calibrate the scoring. Here's what the data showed — and what surprised us.

📊 Pre-launch audit data (40 pages)

Average score: 72.7/100. Not terrible, not great. But the interesting part is where pages break down by category.

The three weakest areas across all 40 pages:
→ CTA — 57% of pages scored below 70. Most CTAs were generic ("Get Started", "Submit") with no clear outcome for the visitor.
→ Social Proof — 57% below 70. Not because pages had bad testimonials — most had none visible above the fold, or none at all.
→ Forms — 42% below 70. And 9 out of 40 pages (22.5%) had zero lead capture form. No email field. No signup. Traffic arrives and leaves with no way to follow up.

The pattern: most pages aren't broken in obvious ways. They're leaking in places founders don't think to check.
