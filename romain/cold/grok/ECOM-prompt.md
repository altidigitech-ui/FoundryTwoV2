# PROMPT GROK — Recherche cibles cold outreach e-com & agences

**Usage :** Prompt FIXE à copier-coller dans Grok. R ouvre Grok, colle ce prompt, Grok retourne les cibles. Ne change quasi jamais.
**Status :** EN PAUSE jusqu'au 13/04/2026 (StoreMD pas encore live)
**Volume attendu :** 3-5 cibles/jour sur Twitter (normal pour cette niche, les merchants Shopify tweetent peu leurs problèmes)

---

## PROMPT À COPIER

```
Find me 10-15 tweets from the last 48 hours from REAL Shopify store owners or e-commerce merchants who are struggling with a business problem. I want people venting, asking for help, or sharing a bad experience — NOT people selling tools, courses, or services.

The kind of problems I'm looking for:
- Store is slow or not converting
- Too many apps causing issues
- Chargebacks or account freezes
- Ads not profitable / ROAS doesn't match actual revenue
- Uninstalled an app and something broke
- Shopify support not helping
- Revenue dropped and they don't know why

Also find 5 tweets from agency owners or freelance marketers complaining about client work (reporting, proving ROI, difficult clients).

CRITICAL RULES:
- The person must be a STORE OWNER or AGENCY OWNER, not someone selling a solution
- 50 to 50,000 followers
- Fewer than 50 replies
- English only
- Last 48 hours

For each tweet give me:
1. URL
2. Author + follower count
3. Replies count
4. What they're struggling with (one line)
5. Best data point to share: speed/app bloat, ghost code after uninstall, phantom billing, bot traffic, chargebacks $800/mo, or client reporting 15h/mo wasted

If you find fewer than 10, tell me what IS trending among Shopify merchants right now so I can adjust.

Exclude these accounts: @FabGangi, @foundrytwo, @oligardner, @copyhackers, @jonbrosio, @thejustinwelsh
```

## NOTES

- Volume réaliste : 3-5 cibles qualifiées par session de 48h. Les merchants Shopify se plaignent davantage sur Reddit et Facebook Groups que sur Twitter.
- Complémenter avec LinkedIn cold (5/jour, recherche manuelle) pour atteindre le volume de 10 outreachs/jour.
- Si Grok retourne < 5 résultats, utiliser les termes alternatifs qu'il propose et relancer.
