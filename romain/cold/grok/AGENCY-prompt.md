# PROMPT GROK — Recherche cibles cold outreach agences/freelancers

**Usage :** Prompt FOCALISÉ agences/freelancers à copier-coller dans Grok. Complémentaire au prompt ECOM. Utiliser quand R veut cibler spécifiquement les agences et freelancers marketing.

---

## PROMPT À COPIER

```
Search Twitter for recent posts (last 48 hours) from marketing agency owners, freelance marketers, digital consultants, and social media managers who are:

- Frustrated with client reporting (manual reports, time wasted)
- Struggling to prove ROI to clients
- Dealing with client churn or pricing issues
- Overwhelmed by managing multiple clients
- Complaining about agency tools or workflow

Search these terms one by one in the "Latest" tab:

- "client reporting" nightmare OR manual OR hours
- "prove ROI" client OR agency
- "agency" scaling pain OR growing pains
- "freelance marketer" burnout OR overwhelmed
- "client churn" agency OR freelance
- "agency reporting" tool OR software OR manual
- "marketing reporting" time wasted
- "agency owner" struggling OR frustrated
- "freelance" client management nightmare

For each relevant post found, give me:

1. URL of the tweet
2. Number of followers of the account
3. Number of replies on the tweet
4. Type: agency owner / freelancer / consultant
5. One line: what they're complaining about

Filter criteria — ONLY include posts that:

- Posted in the last 48 hours
- Account has 50-50,000 followers
- Tweet has fewer than 50 replies
- Post is in English
- Post is about a BUSINESS problem (not code/technical)

EXCLUDE:

- Developers discussing code issues
- Accounts selling competing SaaS tools or CRO/marketing services
- E-commerce merchants (those go in the ECOM prompt)
- Posts from accounts I already follow: @FabGangi, @foundrytwo, @jonbrosio, @thejustinwelsh

Give me 5 to 10 results, sorted by most recent first.
```

---

## NOTES

- Ce prompt est COMPLÉMENTAIRE au prompt ECOM. Utiliser les deux en séquence pour couvrir les 2 verticals de R.
- Les résultats alimentent le cold outreach avec des insights agences (reporting, ROI, client churn, pricing).
