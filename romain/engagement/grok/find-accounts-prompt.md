# PROMPT GROK — Find Active E-com/Marketing Accounts

**Usage :** Coller ce prompt dans Grok pour trouver de nouveaux comptes e-com/marketing à suivre et engager. Lancer 1x/semaine (vendredi ou batch).

---

Search Twitter for the most active and engaged accounts in these niches: Shopify e-commerce, marketing agencies, freelance marketers, e-com conversion, store optimization, client reporting, agency growth, pricing strategy, build in public (business/growth angle).

I want to find accounts that:

1. Post at least 3-5 times per week (high frequency)
2. Get consistent engagement on their posts (10+ replies or 50+ likes regularly)
3. Have between 1,000 and 200,000 followers
4. Post in English
5. Share ACTIONABLE content: e-com data, growth insights, Shopify tips, agency scaling, pricing experiments, conversion metrics, chargeback solutions, client reporting improvements, revenue metrics

Search these terms in the "Top" tab to find them:

* "shopify store" conversion OR growth OR tips
* "ecommerce" marketing strategy OR growth
* "agency" scaling OR client reporting OR ROI
* "freelance marketer" growth OR strategy
* "shopify" chargeback OR speed OR apps
* "pricing" ecommerce experiment OR tested
* "conversion rate" ecommerce OR shopify
* "launched" revenue OR MRR #buildinpublic
* "client retention" agency OR freelance
* "marketing ROI" strategy OR playbook

EXCLUDE:

* Accounts I already follow (see list below)
* Accounts focused on code, architecture, DevOps, backend (not my niche — that's F's territory)
* Accounts focused on crypto, web3, trading
* Accounts that only retweet and never post original content
* Accounts with < 1,000 followers
* Accounts that post generic motivational content without data or actionable insights
* Agencies that only self-promote without sharing real insights

For each account found, give me:

1. @handle
2. Name
3. Number of followers (EXACT — check their profile)
4. Posting frequency (how often they post per week)
5. Average engagement (likes + replies on their recent posts)
6. What they post about (1 line)
7. Their most engaging recent tweet (URL)

Organize results in 2 tables:

**TABLE 1 — HIGH-REACH ACCOUNTS (10K-200K followers)**

E-com influencers, marketing experts, Shopify partners, agency leaders where being seen in the replies builds visibility fast. Give me 5-10 results sorted by engagement rate.

**TABLE 2 — ACTIVE FOUNDERS (1K-10K followers)**

Shopify merchants, agency owners, freelance marketers who share growth data, revenue updates, conversion insights. These become Tier 3 relationships and cold outreach targets. Give me 5-10 results sorted by posting frequency.
